Assassins Creed:The Ezio Trilogy

The Assassin's Creed: The Ezio Collection follows Ezio Auditore da Firenze as he fights to avenge his family and grows into a legendary Assassin. The story-driven gameplay focuses on intense battles, stealth assassinations, and strategic combat against the Templars. Players complete a variety of missions, from infiltrating enemy strongholds to large-scale fights, while progressing through Ezio's journey across Renaissance cities

Far cry 3

This one here is my all time favorite because it gives gamers a massive map environment to explore.Massive map environment meaning that it has mountains (ideal for escaping),jungles for hunting animals/enemies ,towers to scan the areas and most importantly it has small villages or native rural villages which allow driving vehicles.The game allows player freedom meaning you can visit any destination and has a ton of missions that can be completed by stealth for taking down enemies silently,sniping from distance and full action combat. The game has beautiful graphics,landscapes, dynamic weather and wildlife which makes the game cinematic.
